The user wants to build Sous Chef Linguine – The Authentic Global Recipe Engine, a multilingual, AI-powered cooking platform. The core mission is to deliver authentic, traditional, and culturally correct recipes from every country, rejecting Americanized or globalized distortions.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Authenticity First:** A strict, multi-level authenticity framework (Official > Traditional > Modern) must be used to validate every recipe. Validation requires checking native-language sources, country-specific domains, and official registries (DOP, PAT, etc.).
- **Sous Chef Linguine Persona:** All content must be written in the warm, humorous, and sensory voice of an Italian sous-chef. The tone should be culturally rich but concise (max 8-10 lines for introductions).
- **AI-Powered Engines:** The platform must feature several AI engines for:
    - **On-Demand Generation:** Generate recipes automatically when a user searches for one that doesn't exist in the database.
    - **Authenticity Validation:** Rank and validate sources.
    - **Adaptive Cooking Logic:** Adjust cooking times based on weight, cut, and quantity.
    - **Translation & Locale Adaptation:** Display all content in the user's selected language (IT, EN, ES, FR, DE) and adapt units (Metric/Imperial) and temperature (°C/°F).
- **Data Ingestion:** The database must be populated through multiple methods: initial seeding, on-demand generation, official scraping, manual imports (CSV, PDF), and an automated bot.
- **Rich Recipe Structure:** Every recipe must include a detailed structure with history, cultural context, no-no rules, techniques, variations, media (photos, YouTube links), and user-generated content.
- **User & Community Features:** Users must be able to create accounts, save favorite recipes, rate recipes (1-5 stars), and leave comments. A Verified by Community badge system should be implemented.
- **Discovery:** The homepage should feature trending, recently generated, and regionally popular recipes based on analytics like search, view, and favorite counts.
- **Tech Stack:** FastAPI backend, React frontend, and MongoDB database.

**User's preferred language**: English
